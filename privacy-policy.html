Privacy Policy


Thank you for choosing to be part of our community at Combinatorics Technologies Private Limited, doing business as Task Venturers. We are duly committed to protecting your personal data and your right to privacy.

When you visit our website https://www.taskventurers.com/ and use our services, you trust us with your personal information. In this privacy statement, we describe how any personal information you provide on our website is collected and used. Any personal information you provide us is treated according to this policy.

We request you to take some time to read it carefully. If you do not agree with any term in this policy statement, we request you to discontinue the use of our website and our services.

1. WHAT INFORMATION DO WE COLLECT?
1.1 Personal information you disclose to us
We gather all your personal information, which may include your name, address, contact number, passwords, payment data, and other private information.

When you create an online account with us, we collect your personal data that you voluntarily provide to us. Apart from that, we also collect your information when you express interest in obtaining information about us and our services, or participate in activities, surveys, queries, or fill up forms on our website or otherwise contact us.

Your personal information that we collect depends upon how you interact with us, your choice of services you use. To be more precise, your personal information may include the following:

Name and Contact- We collect your first and last name, email address, postal address, phone number, and other similar data that can be used to contact you.

Credentials– We collect your saved passwords, password hints, and similar security information used for authentication and account access provided by you at the time of registration.

Payment Data- Your payment instrument number along with the associated security code, credit card details and any other necessary information related to the payment process are collected by us when you make a purchase online. All payment data is stored by our payment processor PayPal. We suggest you review its corresponding privacy policy and contact the payment processor directly if any question arises.

1.2 Information collected automatically
Your personal data including your IP address and/ or browser and device characteristics is collected automatically whenever you visit our website.

If you use, visit or navigate through our website without revealing your particular identity information such as name, or contact details, some information may still get collected. It may include the information about the usage, IP addresses, browser, and device characteristics, operating systems, language preferences, reference of URLs, name of your device, location, country and other technical information relevant to your access, timing, and usage of the website.

All of this information is primarily needed to maintain the security and operation of our website, internal analytics and reporting purposes. Like any other business, we also collect information through cookies and similar technologies.

1.3 Information collected from other sources
We collect information such as limited data from public databases, marketing partners, social media platforms, and other outside sources. Public databases, marketing partners, social media platforms (such as Facebook, Google, Twitter, etc.), and other third-party services may also provide us with your personal details. To be more precise, the popular social media sites like Facebook, Twitter, Linkedin, Instagram, etc. may prompt us to collect your name, gender, DOB, email address, present location (city, state) and other personal data.

We can also collect user identification numbers for your contacts, profile picture URL and any other information that you have made public. Your marketing leads and search results and links, including paid listings (such as sponsored links), may also be visible to us.

2. YOUR INFORMATIONS AND OUR ACTIVITIES
All the collected information via our website are used for business interests described below. We process your information after we enter into a contract with you, with your consent, and/ or for compliance with our legal obligations. We do indicate the specific processing grounds we rely on next to each purpose listed below.

Sending you marketing and promotional communications- For any marketing purpose, we and our third-party partners may use your personal details relevant to your marketing preferences. You can opt-out of our marketing and promotional emails at any time. You can check the “WHAT ARE YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS” section for more information on this topic.

Sending administrative information to you-
Based on your personal information collected from you, we may send you updates and information about our new products or services, new features or to inform you about new changes made to our policies, terms and/ or conditions.

Fulfilling and managing your orders-
For fulfilling and managing your orders, payments, returns, and exchanges made through the website, we may use your information.

Delivering targeted advertisements to you-
We may use your personal information to develop and display content and display advertising (and work with third parties who do so) according to your interests and to measure its effectiveness.

Requesting Feedback-
We may use your information and contact details to request feedback and to contact you about your use of our website and services.

Protecting our Sites-
As part of our efforts to keep our website safe and secure (for example, for fraud monitoring and prevention) we may use your details.

Responding to legal requests and prevent harm-
If we receive a subpoena or any other legal request, we may need to inspect the data we hold to determine how to respond.

For other Business Purposes-
Sometimes business purposes such as data analysis, identifying usage trends, determining the effectiveness of our promotional and marketing strategies may require is to share users’ personal information.

3. WILL YOUR INFORMATION BE SHARED WITH ANYONE?
With your consent, we share your personal information to fulfill business obligations complying with the law.

Consent:
On your consent only we use your personal information for any specific purpose.

Legitimate Interests:
We may process your data for the requirement of our legitimate business purposes and relevant interests.

Performance of a Contract:
If we have entered into a contract with you, we may process your personal information to fulfill the terms of the contract.

Legal Bindings-
We may be obligated to share your data in order to stay compliant with governmental requests, applicable regulations, a legal process or a judicial proceeding that involves a court order, mostly to respond to the public authorities for meeting legal or security requirements.

Vital Interests:
We may be required to share your details in case it is necessary for prevention, investigation or taking action against policy violation, potential threat, suspected fraud or illegal activity, or in litigation we may be involved.

In the following situations we may share your personal information:

Vendors, Consultants, and Other Third-Party Service Providers-
We will share your information with contractors, service providers, and third-party vendors, that need access to such information. However, the data sharing will be done under strict security compliance. It may involve data analysis, payment processing, hosting services, email delivery, marketing efforts and customer service. Other than that, third-parties may apply tracking technologies for collecting information regarding the interaction between you and the respective website. The purpose of the data collection is to track, analyze and understand your online activity and determine the perception of content. However, we fully assure that your information is not shared, traded or sold to any third-parties for promotional purposes.

Business Transfers-
During negotiations of any merger, sale of company assets, financing, or acquisition of all or a portion of our business to another company, your personal information may be used.
4. WHO WILL YOUR INFORMATION BE SHARED WITH? With your consent, we share your details with third parties we have chosen. Data sharing with any third party is done maintaining the required compliance and security measures. Below is a list of all third-party organizations we have shared your data with. 5. USE OF COOKIES AND OTHER TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES
We may use cookies and other tracking technologies while collecting and saving your personal information in our database.

The cookies, beacons, pixels and other similar technologies that we use collect certain information about you in order to recognize your device and browser, know about your interests and preferences, and be able to provide you with essential functionalities and features on our website.

6. HOW LONG DO WE KEEP YOUR INFORMATION?
Unless required by law, we keep your information for fulfilment of the purposes outlined in this privacy policy. We always try to preserve your personal information unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law (such as tax, accounting or other legal requirements).

After the termination of a user account, we do not keep the user’s information longer than one year as our privacy policy does not require the same. If there is no legitimate business purpose, we do not need to process your data.

We either delete it or anonymize it, or securely store your personal information and isolate it from any further processing until deletion is possible (because your personal information has been stored in backup archives).

7. YOUR INFORMATION IS COMPLETELY SAFE WITH US-
Our aim is to protect your personal information through strict organizational and technical security measures. To protect the privacy and security of your data, our website implements strong security measures. However, we cannot guarantee that your internet is itself 100% secure. Please know that the transmission of your personal information to and from our website is at your own risk. We suggest you to always use a secure environment to access the site and services.

8. DO WE COLLECT INFORMATION FROM MINORS?
We do not collect any personal information from a minor under the age of 18 at any time. We can guarantee that no information from or about children of less than 18 years of age is knowingly solicited by us.

You must confirm that you are at least 18 years of age or that you are the parent or guardian of such a minor and give consent to such minor dependent’s use of our website.

If we come to know that any of the collected personal information is pertaining to users less than 18 years of age, we will immediately deactivate that user account and take necessary actions to omit all the records saved in our servers. If you come across any such data collected from children of minor age, please let us know at info@taskventurers.com.

9. YOUR PRIVACY RIGHTS:
Residents of the European Economic Area, have the rights to access and control their personal information shared with us. A user account can be reviewed, changed, or even deleted at any time.

If you are from the European Economic Area, then you have certain rights under the applicable data protection law. It includes the right

(i) to make a request if access and obtain a copy of your personal information,

(ii) to request rectification or erasure;

(iii) to make you restricted in the processing of your personal information; and

(iv) if applicable, to data portability.

In certain cases, you may have the right to object to the processing of your personal information. To make such a request, please do use the below-mentioned contact details. After consideration, we will act on any such request in accordance with the applicable data protection law.

You have the right to withdraw your consent if you find that we rely on your consent to process your personal information. However, please note that it will not affect the process so far and will not at all negate its lawfulness before its withdrawal.

You have the right to complain to your local data protection supervisory authority without any restriction if you find any possibility or evidence of unlawful processing of your personal information in the European Economic Area.

You may reach us for any further query: https://www.taskventurers.com/contact/

Account deactivation/ termination- If you want to review or change the information in your account or terminate your account at any time, you can either log into your account and change the settings or contact us using the contact details provided. Upon receiving your request to terminate your account, we will immediately start the process of deactivating or deleting your account and information from our active databases. Some information stored in our database can be used to prevent fraud, troubleshoot problems, assist with investigations, enforce our Terms of Use and/ or comply with legal requirements.

Cookies and similar technologies- Most web browsers accept cookies by default. However, you can choose to set your browser to remove cookies or reject those if you prefer. But if you choose to remove or reject cookies, it may cause dysfunctions on certain features or services of our website.

Opting out of email marketing: If you want to stop getting marketing emails and promotional components from our website, you can at any time unsubscribe by either clicking on the UNSUBSCRIBE button residing at the bottom of the emails or simply contacting us using the details provided below. We will immediately unregister your email address from our marketing list. However, you will still receive the service-related emails for administration purposes and for the sake of your account.

10. CONTROLS FOR DO-NOT-TRACK FEATURES
The Do-Not-Track (DNT) features are always available in most web browsers, mobile operating systems, and mobile applications. It can be easily activated to set your privacy preference or if you do not want your online browsing activities tracked and collected.

No uniform technology standard has been finalized for recognizing and implementing the DNT features. Due to that, we may not currently respond to any DNT browser signals that agree with your choice of not being tracked online.

We will inform you about the practice in a revised version of this privacy policy when a standard for online tracking is adopted.

11. DO CALIFORNIA RESIDENTS HAVE SPECIFIC PRIVACY RIGHTS?
Yes, a resident of California, is always granted specific rights regarding access to his/ her personal information.

The California Civil Code, Section 1798.83, “Shine The Light” law permits all our Californian users to request and obtain certain information from us free of charge for once in a year. This information is about his/ her personal data (if any) that we disclosed to third parties for direct marketing purposes along with the names and addresses of all third parties to whom the personal information in the immediately preceding calendar year was shared.

To make such a request, a California resident can easily submit his/ her request in writing to us using the contact information provided in the section below.

A minor user residing in California who does have a registered account on our website has the right to request the removal of unwanted data that he may have previously posted publicly. To do the same, the user needs to get in touch with us via the contact information provided below. We may need the user’s personal email address that is associated with his/ her account and a statement that he/ she does reside in California. We will positively make sure that the data is not publicly displayed on the websites. However, please be aware that the data may not be completely removed from our systems.

12. UPDATION TO THIS POLICY:
Yes, we will always update our privacy policy as needed in order to stay compliant with the relevant laws. The updated versions will be indicated by a “Revised” date and will be effective as soon as it is accessible.

Any changes to this privacy policy will be notified to you either by a prominently posted notice or by directly sending a notification to you.

We request you to review this privacy policy on a frequent basis to be informed about how we are protecting your information.

To know more about our disclaimers, limits, usage, etc., kindly visit our Terms of Service Page.
